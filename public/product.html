How it works

Three primitives. End-to-end proof.

01

Issue Passport

Ed25519-signed credential. Agent identity, scopes, delegation chain.
02

Attest Action

Every action anchored to a SHA-256 Merkle tree. Tamper-evident, append-only.
03

Verify Anywhere

Any third party verifies with only the CA public key. No network call to Vane required.

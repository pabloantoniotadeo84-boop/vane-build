The Problem
Agent actions are unattributable. An incident happens — a financial transaction, a data access, a decision — and you cannot prove which agent acted, under whose authority, within what scope.
Vane Answers
Ed25519-signed passports for every agent. Every action anchored in a SHA-256 Merkle tree. Verification works offline — no round-trip to Vane on the hot path. Audit trail is yours.
The Evidence
273 passing tests. 5 published packages under @vane.build. 200+ weekly npm downloads. Live infrastructure on Railway. Cross-organizational by design — nobody else owns this.
